## How to Customize & Print

1. **Download immediately** after purchase from your Etsy account.
2. Open the PDF in **Adobe Acrobat Reader** (free download at adobe.com).
3. Click each text field to enter the graduate's name, school, degree/program, party date, time, and location.
4. Save your completed file, then choose your printing path:
   - **Print at home** on cardstock for a quick, personal touch.
   - **Upload to an online printer** (Canva Print, Vistaprint, Shutterfly) for professional results shipped to your door.
   - **Send digitally** — export as PNG and share via text, email, or Evite.

No Canva subscription required, though the PNG works perfectly if you prefer Canva's editor.

## Printing Tips

- **Cardstock weight:** 80–110 lb gives invitations the right feel and weight.
- **Bleed area:** The design includes a 0.125" bleed on all sides — leave it in place when uploading to print shops.
- **Digital sending:** Export or screenshot the PNG at full resolution for crisp results on any screen.
- For **bulk printing**, most local print shops (Staples, FedEx, Office Depot) can print 25–50 invitations same-day from the PDF file.
