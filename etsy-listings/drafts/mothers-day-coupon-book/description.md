## Printing Tips

- **Best results:** Print on cardstock (65–110 lb) for a sturdy coupon that feels like a real gift.
- **Color printers** produce the most vibrant results, but the design also prints beautifully in black and white.
- **Print shop tip:** Upload the PDF as-is. Request "actual size" or "100%" — do not scale to fit.
- Laminating the cover page gives it an extra-special, keepsake feel.
