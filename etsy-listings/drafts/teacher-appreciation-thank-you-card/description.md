## Printing Tips

- **Paper:** White or cream **cardstock (65–80 lb)** gives the card a premium, sturdy feel. Regular printer paper works in a pinch.
- **Home printer:** Select **"actual size"** in your print settings. Both inkjet and laser printers produce great results.
- **Print shop:** Bring the PDF to FedEx Office, Staples, or Walgreens Photo for crisp color output.
- **Folding:** Print on one side and fold in half for a classic greeting card format.
