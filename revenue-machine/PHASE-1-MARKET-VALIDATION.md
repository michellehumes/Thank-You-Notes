| Single freelance contract | $8-$15 | 3-8 pages | 50-500 | No system, just one doc |
| Invoice template | $5-$12 | 1-3 pages | 100-1000 | Standalone, no context |

| Business planner | $12-$25 | 40-100 pages | 500-5000 | Generic, not freelancer-specific |
| Business starter kit | $15-$30 | 20-50 pages | 100-800 | Too surface-level |

### Competitor Weaknesses
1. **Fragmented**: Buyers must purchase 5-8 items from different sellers to get complete coverage
2. **Generic**: Most templates are "one-size-fits-all" — not tailored to freelancer workflows
3. **Design over function**: Pretty but not deeply practical or filled with actionable guidance
4. **Missing finance tools**: Most skip profit tracking, tax prep, and pricing strategy
5. **No instruction**: Templates with zero guidance on how to use them effectively

### Our Competitive Advantage
- **All-in-one system**: Everything a freelancer needs in one purchase
- **Operational depth**: Not just templates — includes guidance, formulas, and strategy
- **Three formats**: Printable PDF + Fillable PDF + Canva editable
- **120+ pages**: 2x-3x more content than any competitor at similar price
- **Actionable**: Every section includes how-to context, not just blank fields
