## CANVA TEMPLATE STRUCTURE INSTRUCTIONS

### Design System
- **Primary Font**: Montserrat (headers) — clean, modern, professional
- **Secondary Font**: Open Sans (body) — excellent readability
- **Accent Font**: Playfair Display (titles, covers only) — premium feel
- **Color Palette**:
  - Primary: #1A1A2E (Dark Navy) — authority
  - Secondary: #16213E (Deep Blue) — trust
  - Accent: #E94560 (Coral Red) — energy, CTAs
  - Highlight: #F5C518 (Gold) — premium feel
  - Background: #FAFAFA (Off-white) — clean
  - Text: #333333 (Dark Gray) — readability
- **Spacing**: 24px margins, 16px between sections, 8px between elements
- **Grid**: 2-column layout for trackers, full-width for guides
- **Icons**: Minimal line icons only — avoid clip art
- **Hierarchy**: H1 (28pt) > H2 (22pt) > H3 (18pt) > Body (12pt) > Small (10pt)

### Cover Mockup Concept
- Dark navy background with subtle geometric pattern
- Gold accent line across top third
- Product title in white Montserrat Bold, large
- Subtitle in coral red
- "120+ Pages | 8 Modules | Fillable PDFs" badge
- Mock device display showing internal pages fanned out
- Clean, minimal, premium aesthetic — NOT busy or cluttered
