# Shelzy's Designs — ChatGPT 4o Etsy Image Prompt Library

**Version:** March 2026
**Brand:** Shelzy's Designs
**Colors:** White #fefefe · Pink #fb5887 · Orange #fe8c43 · Blue #3ca4d7 · Teal #8adbde
**Fonts:** Berthold Block (titles) · Montserrat Bold (headers) · Inter (body)
**Spec:** 2700×2025px, 4:3 ratio, 300 DPI
**Style:** Clean, modern, airy, professional ecommerce product photography

---

## MASTER TEMPLATE — Adapt per product by swapping `[PRODUCT NAME]` and `[FEATURES]`

All prompts use this brand system:
- Background: pure white (#fefefe), airy and uncluttered
- Accent colors: pink (#fb5887), orange (#fe8c43), blue (#3ca4d7), or teal (#8adbde) — use 1–2 accents per image, not all four
- Props: minimal white desk surface, optional single small plant (white pot), pen, or coffee mug in soft-focus background
- No people. No clutter. No shadows heavier than soft drop shadow.
- All text overlays: bold modern sans-serif, white or dark navy on colored backgrounds
- Image dimensions: 2700×2025px at 300 DPI, 4:3 aspect ratio

## USAGE NOTES

**To adapt for a new product:**
1. Copy the master template section for the relevant image type
2. Replace `[PRODUCT NAME]` with the actual product name
3. Replace `[ACCENT COLOR]` with the primary brand color for this product category:
   - Budget/Finance products → Pink #fb5887
   - Wedding products → Blue #3ca4d7
   - Business products → Orange #fe8c43
   - ADHD/Planner products → Teal #8adbde
4. Replace `[FEATURES]`, `[PERSONAS]`, column names, and step descriptions
5. Paste into ChatGPT 4o with the note: "Use this exact prompt. Do not deviate from colors or dimensions."

**Batch workflow:**
- Generate all 10 prompts for a product in one session
- Ask ChatGPT to generate them in sequence: "Generate image 1, then I'll ask for image 2..."
- Save outputs directly to the product's image folder
