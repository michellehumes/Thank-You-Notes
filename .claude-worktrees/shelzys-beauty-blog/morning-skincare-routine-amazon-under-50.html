A Note on Routine Order

Skincare product order matters because layering in the wrong sequence can prevent absorption or reduce effectiveness. The rule is thinnest to thickest: water-based cleansers and serums go on first, then heavier moisturizers, then SPF last so nothing disrupts its protective film.

The one thing beginners get wrong most often: applying SPF before moisturizer. SPF should always be the final skincare step. Anything applied over it dilutes the protective layer and reduces the effective SPF you're actually getting.
