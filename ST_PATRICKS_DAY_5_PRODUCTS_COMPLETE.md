## ETSY TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS REFERENCE

### Listing Photos
- **Dimensions:** 2000 x 2000px (square, recommended) or 2000 x 1500px (landscape)
- **File format:** JPG, PNG
- **File size:** Under 1MB per image
- **Color space:** sRGB
- **Max photos:** 10 per listing (use all 10)
- **First photo:** Most critical — this is the thumbnail in search results

### Listing Video
- **Length:** 5–15 seconds (no audio on Etsy)
- **Resolution:** 1080p minimum (1920x1080 or 1080x1920)
- **Format:** MP4 (preferred), MOV
- **Max file size:** 100MB
- **Impact:** Listings with video get 2x more orders (per Etsy)

### Best-Performing Photo Types for Digital Downloads
1. **Lifestyle mockup** — product shown in real-world setting (framed on wall, held by hand, on desk)
2. **Flat lay overview** — all items in the bundle spread out
3. **Close-up detail** — showing quality/resolution of the design
4. **Device mockup** — shown on tablet/phone/laptop screen
5. **What's included** — visual checklist of everything in the bundle
6. **Size/format info** — showing file types and print sizes
7. **Before/after or use case** — showing the product being used
8. **Testimonial/social proof** — overlay of reviews or star ratings
9. **Comparison** — why yours is better (quality, quantity, editability)
10. **Instructional** — "How to download & use" steps
