FAQ

Common questions

How is this different from a $50 resume service?
Commodity resume services use templates and generic keyword lists. We analyze your specific target job descriptions, extract the exact keywords and qualifications they're screening for, and rebuild your resume to match. Every achievement is quantified. Every bullet is optimized. You also get a strategy document explaining what was changed and why, so you can adapt your materials going forward.
What's your turnaround time?
48 hours from the time you submit your intake form with all materials (current resume, job descriptions, career goals). Rush delivery (24 hours) is available for an additional $99.
What if I don't like the result?
Every tier includes revision rounds (1-3 depending on your tier). If revisions don't resolve your concerns, you get a full refund within 7 days of delivery. No questions asked. We've never had a refund request, but the guarantee is there so you can invest with confidence.
Do you use AI?
We use AI-powered tools to analyze job descriptions, extract keywords, and identify gaps — this makes us faster and more thorough than manual-only services. But every deliverable is reviewed, refined, and quality-checked by a career strategist. You get the speed of AI with the judgment of a human expert.
What format will I receive?
You'll receive your resume in both PDF and DOCX format. The DOCX is fully editable so you can make future tweaks. Both formats are ATS-safe — single column, no tables, no graphics in the header, standard fonts. LinkedIn content is delivered as plain text you can copy-paste directly.
I'm changing industries. Can you help?
Yes. Career pivots are one of our specialties. For a full industry change, we recommend Tier 2 or Tier 3, which include multiple tailored versions so you can position yourself differently for different types of roles. We also offer a Career Pivot Rewrite add-on for deeper repositioning.
How do revisions work?
After you receive your deliverables, you have 14 days to request revisions. Send us your feedback — specific is better — and we'll deliver the revised version within 24 hours. Each tier includes a set number of revision rounds (Tier 1: 1, Tier 2: 2, Tier 3: 3).
Is my information kept confidential?
Absolutely. We do not share, sell, or publish any client information or materials. Your resume, job descriptions, and personal details are used solely to create your deliverables and are deleted 30 days after project completion.
