## Page 2 — How to Use This Book

```
HOW TO USE THIS BOOK

This journal was created to give you — or your loved one — one organized place
to track every step of cancer treatment. Fill in the Treatment Overview on the
next page as soon as you receive your diagnosis and care plan. Before each chemo
cycle, review what to expect and have your questions ready on a fresh Cycle Log.
During and after infusion, log your medications, side effects, and how you feel.
Bring this journal to every appointment so you never lose a doctor's answer again.
Your story, your treatment, your record — all in one place.
```
