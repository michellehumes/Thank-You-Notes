# Cover Design Brief

**KDP trim size:** 6x9
**Page count:** 120
**Spine width formula:** (120 x 0.0025) + 0.06 = 0.360 inches

**Style:** Warm, supportive, hopeful — not clinical, not cold, not a medical binder. This should feel like a gift from someone who cares. Avoid stark white clinical aesthetics and overly somber dark palettes.
**Color palette:**
  - #4A7C8E — Calm teal/slate blue (primary, grounding)
  - #F5EFE6 — Warm cream (background panels, clean space)
  - #D4A5A5 — Soft rose/dusty pink (gentle accent, warmth)
  - #8BAF9A — Sage green (secondary accent, hope)

**Typography:**
  - Title font: Montserrat Bold
  - Subtitle font: Inter Regular

**Cover imagery:** A single blooming flower — a peony, ranunculus, or lotus — rendered in soft watercolor style against the warm cream or teal background. The flower should feel alive and hopeful, not funereal. Search Canva stock: "watercolor peony soft" or "lotus flower watercolor warm". Alternative: abstract watercolor wash in teal and rose tones with no figurative image — letting color do the emotional work. Avoid ribbons (overly branded), stethoscopes, pills, or overly medical imagery.

**Required text:**
  - Title: Cancer Treatment Journal
  - Subtitle: Chemo Cycles, Doctor Q&A & Treatment Tracker
  - Author: Shelzy Perkins

**Back cover text:**
```
For anyone navigating cancer treatment —
and for the people walking beside them.

• Chemo cycle logs with pre-meds, infusion drugs & reaction fields
• Side effect severity tracker (nausea, fatigue, neuropathy & more)
• Doctor appointment Q&A record — never lose an answer again
• Temperature and hydration logs for high-risk post-chemo days
• Treatment overview card for your full care team & protocol
• Emotional check-in pages — a space that's just yours

120 pages | 6x9 inches | Paperback
```

**Canva steps:** Search '6x9 book cover' in Canva, select blank template, set canvas to 12.36 x 9 inches (front + spine + back), apply warm cream background to front panel, place watercolor flower imagery, add teal title bar with Montserrat Bold title text, add subtitle in Inter Regular, author name in sage green or cream below.
