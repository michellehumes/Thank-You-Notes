# Book Brief: Fertility TTC Tracker

**Niche:** Fertility TTC Tracker
**Buyer persona:** Women aged 25–40 who are actively trying to conceive (TTC), whether naturally or alongside fertility treatments like IUI or IVF. They're highly engaged researchers who consume fertility content constantly — charting basal body temperature, tracking cervical mucus, using OPKs, and logging cycle data obsessively. Many are on month 3–18 of trying and deeply motivated. They also gift these to friends who've just started TTC.
**Core promise:** The most complete TTC tracking tool in paperback — one spread per cycle day with BBT charting, OPK results, cervical mucus, symptoms, medications, and intercourse timing all in one place, so nothing gets lost between apps.
**Format:** 6x9 paperback, 120 pages
**Paperback price:** $8.99
**Kindle price:** $3.99
**Etsy crossover:** YES -- printable TTC trackers are among the top-selling Etsy digital downloads in the planner category; a printable bundle (monthly cycle chart + daily log pages) would sell strongly
