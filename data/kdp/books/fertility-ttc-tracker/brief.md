# Book Brief: Fertility + TTC Tracker (BBT, OPK, cycle log)

**Niche:** Fertility + TTC Tracker (BBT, OPK, cycle log)
**Buyer persona:** Women aged 25–40 who are actively trying to conceive and want to chart their basal body temperature, ovulation predictor kit results, cervical mucus, and cycle data in one organized book. They may be in their first few months of TTC or have been at it for over a year; many are coordinating with a reproductive endocrinologist or OB-GYN and need a paper record. They buy this because digital apps feel clinical and impersonal, and because having a physical chart to bring to appointments feels empowering. Often purchased as a hopeful self-gift or by a supportive partner.
**Core promise:** The most complete paper fertility chart available -- BBT graphing, OPK tracking, cycle mapping, and appointment notes in one thoughtfully designed book.
**Format:** 6x9 paperback, 100 pages
**Paperback price:** $9.99
**Kindle price:** $4.99
**Etsy crossover:** YES -- individual monthly cycle chart pages sell extremely well as Etsy printables; this is a direct crossover product.
