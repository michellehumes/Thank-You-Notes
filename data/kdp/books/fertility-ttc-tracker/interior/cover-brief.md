# Cover Design Brief: Fertility & TTC Tracker

**KDP trim size:** 6x9
**Page count:** 120
**Spine width formula:** (120 x 0.0025) + 0.06 = 0.36 inches

**Style:** Warm, hopeful, feminine — not clinical. This book is bought in an emotionally charged season of life. The cover should feel like a gentle encouragement, not a medical device.
**Color palette:**
  - `#E8A598` — Dusty blush rose (warmth, femininity, hope)
  - `#F5EDE8` — Warm cream (background, soft and welcoming)
  - `#8B5E52` — Muted terracotta (grounding accent)
  - `#2C2C2C` — Near-black (title typography contrast)

**Typography:**
  - Title font: Montserrat Bold
  - Subtitle font: Inter Regular

**Cover imagery:** A delicate watercolor-style illustration of a blooming flower (magnolia or cherry blossom preferred — symbolizes new beginnings) with soft botanical leaves, in dusty pink and cream tones. Alternatively, a minimal illustration of a uterus/ovary in a tasteful, artistic style (like the popular anatomy art trend). No cartoonish imagery. No stock photos of pregnant women or babies — the buyer is still trying. The imagery should suggest hope and natural cycles without pressure.

**Required text:**
  - Title: Fertility & TTC Tracker
  - Subtitle: Complete Cycle Charting Journal for BBT, OPKs, Cervical Mucus & Ovulation — Covers 3+ Cycles
  - Author: Shelzy Perkins

**Canva steps:**
1. Search "6x9 book cover" in Canva, select blank template.
2. Set background to `#F5EDE8`.
3. Place botanical watercolor illustration in the center-upper portion of the cover.
4. Add a soft blush `#E8A598` band or frame element to anchor the design.
5. Place title "Fertility & TTC Tracker" in Montserrat Bold, `#2C2C2C`, centered (approx. 40–44pt).
6. Add subtitle in Inter Regular, `#8B5E52`, centered, 14–15pt, below title.
7. Add "Shelzy Perkins" in Inter Regular, 12pt, `#8B5E52`, bottom center.
8. For spine (0.36"): `#E8A598` background, "Fertility & TTC Tracker — Shelzy Perkins" in `#2C2C2C` Montserrat Bold rotated 90°.
9. Back cover: `#F5EDE8` background, 3-line description, barcode placeholder bottom right.
10. Export full wrap as PDF Print (with bleeds) for KDP upload.
