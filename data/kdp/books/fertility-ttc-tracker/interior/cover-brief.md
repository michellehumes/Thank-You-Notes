# Cover Design Brief

**KDP trim size:** 6x9
**Page count:** 100
**Spine width formula:** (100 x 0.0025) + 0.06 = 0.31 inches

**Style:** Warm, hopeful, feminine -- conveys tenderness and optimism without being cutesy or infantile. Appeals to a woman who is serious about her fertility journey but wants to hold space for hope.
**Color palette:**
  - #FDF6F0 Warm Blush Cream (front cover background)
  - #C4856A Terracotta Rose (title bar, spine accent)
  - #8BAF9A Sage Green (subtitle text, botanical accent elements)
  - #3D2B1F Deep Espresso (body text, author name)

**Typography:**
  - Title font: Montserrat Bold
  - Subtitle font: Inter Regular

**Cover imagery:** A soft, top-down lifestyle photo of a small round wooden tray holding: a basal body thermometer, a small glass vial or OPK test strip, a sprig of eucalyptus, and a folded linen cloth. Warm natural light from the left. No people. Neutral linen or white background. Conveys intention and care. Canva stock search terms: "basal thermometer wellness flat lay", "fertility planning aesthetic", "linen tray eucalyptus overhead". Alternatively, use ChatGPT Images: "Warm flat lay photo of a small wooden tray with a digital thermometer, OPK strip, sprig of eucalyptus, and white linen on a cream background. Top-down view, soft golden natural light. No text. Cozy, intentional, hopeful aesthetic."

**Required text:**
  - Title: Fertility & TTC Tracker
  - Subtitle: BBT Chart, OPK Log, Cycle Mapping & Appointment Notes for Trying to Conceive
  - Author: Shelzy Perkins

**Back cover copy (3-4 sentences):**
Your fertility journey deserves more than a phone app. This tracker gives you a dedicated paper log for basal body temperature charting, OPK test results, cervical mucus observations, and daily cycle notes -- organized by cycle, with a built-in BBT graph grid so you can see your pattern at a glance. Bring it to every RE or OB-GYN appointment and walk in with a complete, handwritten record of your body. 100 pages | 6x9 paperback | Shelzy Perkins

**Canva steps:** Search '6x9 book cover' in Canva, select blank template (6 x 9 in). Set front cover background to #FDF6F0. Place stock image centered in upper 50% of front cover. Add a horizontal band at 52% from top (1.25 in tall) in #C4856A. Set title "Fertility & TTC Tracker" in Montserrat Bold 34pt #FDF6F0, centered on band. Below band: subtitle in Inter Regular 11pt #8BAF9A. Author name at bottom in Inter Regular 11pt #3D2B1F. Spine: #C4856A background with title rotated 90° in Montserrat Bold 10pt #FDF6F0. Back cover: #FDF6F0 background, back cover copy in Inter Regular 10pt #3D2B1F, centered, top third.
