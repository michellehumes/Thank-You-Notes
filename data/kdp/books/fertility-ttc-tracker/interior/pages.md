## Page 2: How to Use This Book

```
How to Use This Tracker

Start each cycle on a fresh two-page spread. Record your basal body
temperature (BBT) every morning before getting out of bed -- use the
same thermometer at the same time each day for accuracy. Log your
OPK test result, cervical mucus observation, and any symptoms or
notes in the daily columns. Use the BBT graph grid to plot your
temperature and visually identify your ovulation pattern. Bring this
book to every fertility appointment so your doctor or RE can review
your full cycle history without relying on an app export.
```

## Page 3: Quick Reference Card

```
CYCLE PHASES AT A GLANCE

MENSTRUAL (Days 1–5, avg.)
  Body: Uterine lining sheds. Estrogen and progesterone low.
  BBT: Low, typically 97.0–97.4°F / 36.1–36.4°C
  OPK: Negative (expected)
  Mucus: Little or none; spotting

FOLLICULAR (Days 1–13, avg., overlaps with menstrual)
  Body: FSH rises; follicles develop; estrogen climbs.
  BBT: Low, gradually rising toward ovulation
  OPK: Negative → approaching positive near ovulation
  Mucus: Dry → sticky → creamy → wet

OVULATION (Day 14, avg. -- varies 11–21)
  Body: LH surge triggers egg release. Peak fertility window.
  BBT: Slight dip, then rises 0.2–0.5°F / 0.1–0.3°C after O
  OPK: Peak positive (line as dark or darker than control)
  Mucus: Egg-white cervical mucus (EWCM) -- clear, stretchy

LUTEAL (Days 15–28, avg.)
  Body: Progesterone dominates. Egg travels to uterus.
  BBT: Elevated (stays high if pregnant; drops before period)
  OPK: Negative
  Mucus: Dry or creamy

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────

OPK RESULT KEY
  Negative (-)  -- Test line lighter than control line
  Almost (+)    -- Test line close to control, not quite equal
  Positive (+)  -- Test line as dark as or darker than control (LH surge)
  Peak          -- Darkest positive; ovulation expected within 12–36 hrs

CERVICAL MUCUS KEY
  D  = Dry (no mucus)
  S  = Sticky (white/yellow, breaks when stretched)
  C  = Creamy (lotion-like, white/cloudy)
  W  = Watery (clear, thin)
  E  = Egg-white (EWCM -- clear, stretchy 1+ inch)
  B  = Bleeding / spotting

BBT NORMAL RANGES
  Pre-ovulation (low phase):  97.0–97.7°F  /  36.1–36.5°C
  Post-ovulation (high phase): 97.8–98.6°F  /  36.6–37.0°C
  Thermal shift: rise of ≥0.2°F above your average low-phase BBT
```
