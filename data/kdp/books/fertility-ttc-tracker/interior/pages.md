## Page 2 — How to Use This Book

```
HOW TO USE THIS JOURNAL

This tracker is designed around your menstrual cycle, not the
calendar month. Start a new Cycle Overview Chart when your period
begins (Cycle Day 1). Then complete one Daily Log entry for each
day of your cycle until your next period begins.

For best results with BBT charting: take your temperature at the
same time every morning before getting out of bed, ideally using a
basal thermometer that reads to two decimal places. Record it
immediately — even a few minutes of movement affects the reading.

Bring this journal to fertility appointments. Your RE or OB/GYN
can identify anovulatory cycles, luteal phase defects, and
patterns that apps can miss when they see your raw data.

[tip box, light border]
TIP: Use a consistent symbol for "intercourse" (e.g., a star ★)
in the Notes column. This makes timing patterns easy to see at
a glance when reviewing cycles.
```

## Page 3 — Quick Reference Card: Cycle Phases & Fertility Signs

```
QUICK REFERENCE: FERTILITY CHARTING GUIDE
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

YOUR CYCLE AT A GLANCE
  Menstruation   │ Day 1–5 (avg)  │ Uterine lining sheds
  Follicular     │ Day 1–13 (avg) │ Follicles develop; estrogen rises
  Ovulation      │ Day 14 (avg)   │ LH surge → egg released (12–24 hrs)
  Luteal Phase   │ Day 15–28 (avg)│ Progesterone rises; 12–16 days

  Note: "Average" is based on a 28-day cycle. Your cycle may vary.
  Track YOUR patterns — they're more accurate than any average.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
CERVICAL MUCUS (CM) KEY
  D  │ Dry — no mucus, low fertility
  S  │ Sticky — white/yellow, low-medium fertility
  C  │ Creamy — lotion-like, medium fertility
  W  │ Watery — clear, wet, higher fertility
  EW │ Egg White — stretchy, clear, PEAK fertility
  P  │ Period / Spotting

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
BBT SHIFT INDICATOR
  Pre-ovulation temps are typically LOWER (97.0°–97.7°F / 36.1°–36.5°C)
  Post-ovulation temps RISE by at least 0.2°F and STAY elevated
  A sustained rise of 3+ days typically confirms ovulation has occurred

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
OPK (OVULATION PREDICTOR KIT) GUIDE
  Negative (–) │ Test line lighter than control line
  Positive (+) │ Test line as dark or darker than control line
  Peak         │ Darkest positive — ovulation typically 24–48 hrs later

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
TWW SYMPTOMS TRACKER SHORTHAND (use in notes)
  AF  │ Aunt Flow (period)      │ CP  │ Cervical position
  BFN │ Big fat negative        │ BFP │ Big fat positive
  DPO │ Days past ovulation     │ LP  │ Luteal phase
  BD  │ Baby dance (intercourse)│ TW  │ Two week wait
```
