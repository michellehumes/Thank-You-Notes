# Book Brief: Medication Log Book (daily meds, dosage, refills)

**Niche:** Medication Log Book (daily meds, dosage, refills)
**Buyer persona:** Adults managing 3+ daily medications -- typically those with chronic conditions (diabetes, heart disease, autoimmune disorders, epilepsy) or older adults managing complex medication regimens -- and the family caregivers who manage medications for elderly parents or a chronically ill spouse. They are motivated by safety: missed doses, dangerous interactions, and refill chaos are real daily stressors. They want a simple, durable paper log that works even when phones are dead and apps crash. Frequently purchased as a practical gift by adult children for aging parents.
**Core promise:** The clearest, most caregiver-friendly medication log available -- organized by medication, with daily dose tracking, refill reminders, and pharmacy notes all in one place.
**Format:** 6x9 paperback, 100 pages
**Paperback price:** $7.99
**Kindle price:** $3.99
**Etsy crossover:** NO -- medication logs work poorly as printables because users want a bound, durable book they can keep at the bedside or in a go-bag; binding is part of the value proposition.
