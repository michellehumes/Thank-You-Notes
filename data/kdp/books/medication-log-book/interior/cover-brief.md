# Cover Design Brief

**KDP trim size:** 6x9
**Page count:** 100
**Spine width formula:** (100 x 0.0025) + 0.06 = 0.31 inches

**Style:** Clean, modern, clinical-but-approachable -- conveys reliability, safety, and organization. This cover should communicate immediately that this is a functional reference tool, not a soft wellness journal. Buyer must trust it enough to depend on it.
**Color palette:**
  - #F0F4F8 Light Cool Gray (front cover background)
  - #1B4F72 Navy Blue (title bar, spine, primary text)
  - #E8F5E9 Pale Mint (subtitle accent, secondary graphic element)
  - #FFFFFF White (rule lines, box elements)

**Typography:**
  - Title font: Montserrat Bold
  - Subtitle font: Inter Regular

**Cover imagery:** A clean, organized flat-lay photo of a weekly pill organizer (7-day, clear plastic), two prescription bottles with labels facing away, and a fine-point black pen on a plain white or light gray surface. No clutter. Overhead view, sharp focus, soft shadow. Conveys dependability and medical organization. Canva stock search terms: "pill organizer flat lay", "prescription medication organized desk", "weekly pillbox overhead". Alternatively, ChatGPT Images prompt: "Clean overhead flat lay photo of a 7-day pill organizer, two prescription bottles with plain labels, and a black ballpoint pen on a pure white surface. Sharp detail, minimal shadow, no text, clinical and organized aesthetic."

**Required text:**
  - Title: Medication Log Book
  - Subtitle: Daily Tracker for Medications, Dosages, Refills & Pharmacy Information
  - Author: Shelzy Perkins

**Back cover copy (3-4 sentences):**
Managing multiple medications every day means one missed dose or forgotten refill can have real consequences. This log book gives you a clear, durable paper record for every medication you take -- dose times, refill dates, pharmacy contacts, and 40 weeks of daily check-off tracking, all in one place. Designed for patients and caregivers alike, it works even when your phone is dead and your app is unavailable. 100 pages | 6x9 paperback | Shelzy Perkins

**Canva steps:** Search '6x9 book cover' in Canva, select blank template (6 x 9 in). Set front cover background to #F0F4F8. Place stock image in upper 50% of front cover. Add a solid horizontal bar at 52% from top (1.5 in tall) in #1B4F72. Set title "Medication Log Book" in Montserrat Bold 36pt #FFFFFF, centered on bar. Below bar: subtitle in Inter Regular 11pt #1B4F72. Author name at bottom in Inter Regular 11pt #1B4F72. Optional: add a thin #E8F5E9 horizontal rule (2pt) between subtitle and author. Spine: #1B4F72 background, title rotated 90° in Montserrat Bold 10pt #FFFFFF. Back cover: #F0F4F8 background, back cover copy in Inter Regular 10pt #1B4F72, centered, top third.
