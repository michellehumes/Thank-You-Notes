# Cover Design Brief

**KDP trim size:** 6x9
**Page count:** 100
**Spine width formula:** (100 x 0.0025) + 0.06 = 0.310 inches

**Style:** Clean, modern, slightly warm — professional but approachable. Feels like a quality household reference book, not a cheap notepad.
**Color palette:**
  - #1B3A5C — Deep navy (primary background / title bar)
  - #F8F4EE — Warm white / cream (text panels, clean space)
  - #8FAE8B — Sage green (accent elements, icon color)
  - #C9A84C — Warm gold (borders, detail lines, author name)

**Typography:**
  - Title font: Montserrat Bold
  - Subtitle font: Inter Regular

**Cover imagery:** Clean modern house exterior — simple line-art illustration of a house with a few key icons (wrench, lightbulb, water droplet) arranged neatly beneath or beside the title. Alternatively, Canva stock photo: "modern house exterior minimal" or "blueprint house line art". The image should feel organized and purposeful, not DIY/rustic. Option B: flat-lay of a clipboard, measuring tape, and pen on a navy or warm wood surface.

**Required text:**
  - Title: Home Maintenance Log Book
  - Subtitle: Annual Tracker for Repairs, Upgrades & Seasonal Tasks
  - Author: Shelzy Perkins

**Back cover text:**
```
Your home is your biggest investment.
This log helps you protect it.

• Repair & maintenance log with contractor, cost & warranty details
• Annual seasonal maintenance checklists for all 4 seasons
• Appliance & systems inventory with serial numbers & warranties
• Home profile card with utility accounts & emergency contacts
• 44 maintenance event spreads across 100 pages

Perfect for new homeowners, rental property owners & anyone
who wants a permanent paper record of every repair and upgrade.

100 pages | 6x9 inches | Paperback
```

**Canva steps:** Search '6x9 book cover' in Canva, select blank template, set canvas to 12.31 x 9 inches (front + spine + back), apply navy background to front panel, place house imagery or line-art icon set, add title/subtitle/author in specified fonts and gold/cream colors.
