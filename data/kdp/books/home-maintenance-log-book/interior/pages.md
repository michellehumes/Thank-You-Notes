## Page 2 — How to Use This Book

```
HOW TO USE THIS BOOK

This log is your permanent home record — one place to document every repair,
improvement, contractor visit, appliance purchase, and seasonal maintenance task.
Use the Home Profile section to capture your property details at a glance. Fill in
the Appliance Inventory before your first repair entry so model and serial numbers
are always at hand. Record each maintenance event in the Repair & Maintenance Log
as it happens, and use the Annual Seasonal Checklist each year to stay ahead of
preventive upkeep. Keep this book with your home files or in a kitchen drawer so
it's always accessible when a contractor calls or you need proof of repairs at resale.
```
