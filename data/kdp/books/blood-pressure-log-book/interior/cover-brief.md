# Cover Design Brief: Blood Pressure Log Book

**KDP trim size:** 6x9
**Page count:** 104
**Spine width formula:** (104 x 0.0025) + 0.06 = 0.32 inches

**Style:** Clean, trustworthy, clinical-light — approachable for seniors and caregivers. Professional but not intimidating. Similar feel to what you'd see at a pharmacy display.
**Color palette:**
  - `#C0392B` — Deep calm red (health/heart association, accent only — not alarming)
  - `#FFFFFF` — White (background, open and clean)
  - `#2C3E50` — Deep navy (primary typography)
  - `#ECF0F1` — Light silver-gray (secondary background panels)

**Typography:**
  - Title font: Montserrat Bold
  - Subtitle font: Inter Regular

**Cover imagery:** A simple, clean illustration of a blood pressure monitor cuff (sphygmomanometer) rendered in a flat vector style, in navy and red on white. Alternatively, a clean heart icon with a pulse/EKG line extending from it, minimal and modern. No photographs. No stock images of people. The image should communicate "organized health tracking" at a glance. Keep the design uncluttered so the large title text is the dominant element for shoppers scanning search results on small screens.

**Required text:**
  - Title: Blood Pressure Log Book
  - Subtitle: Daily Home Monitoring Record for Systolic, Diastolic & Pulse — 1 Year of Readings
  - Author: Shelzy Perkins

**Canva steps:**
1. Search "6x9 book cover" in Canva, select blank template.
2. Set background to `#FFFFFF`.
3. Add a bold `#C0392B` horizontal stripe at the top (~1 inch), and a matching thin stripe at the bottom.
4. Place title "Blood Pressure Log Book" in Montserrat Bold, `#2C3E50`, centered, large (approx. 44–48pt).
5. Add subtitle in Inter Regular, `#2C3E50`, centered, 16pt.
6. Place blood pressure monitor or heart + EKG illustration in the center of the cover.
7. Add "Shelzy Perkins" in Inter Regular, 13pt, bottom center, `#2C3E50`.
8. For spine (0.32"): White background, "Blood Pressure Log Book — Shelzy Perkins" in `#2C3E50` Montserrat Bold rotated 90°.
9. Back cover: `#ECF0F1` background, 2-line description + "Shelzy's Designs", barcode placeholder bottom right.
10. Export full wrap as PDF Print (with bleeds) for KDP upload.
