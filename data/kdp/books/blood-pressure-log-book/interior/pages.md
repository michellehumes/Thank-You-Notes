## Page 2 — How to Use This Book

```
HOW TO USE THIS LOG

Record your blood pressure readings at the same time each day for
the most accurate results. Most doctors recommend measuring in the
morning before medications and in the evening before bed. Sit quietly
for five minutes before measuring, and rest your arm on a flat surface.

Bring this log to every doctor appointment. Your healthcare provider
can see trends over weeks or months that a single office reading cannot
capture. A single high reading is not cause for alarm — patterns are
what matter.

This log holds approximately one year of daily readings
(morning + evening) on 100 tracking pages.

[box with light border]
IMPORTANT: This log is for personal record-keeping only.
Always follow your doctor's guidance on medication and treatment.
```
