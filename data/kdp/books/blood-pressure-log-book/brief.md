# Book Brief: Blood Pressure Log Book

**Niche:** Blood Pressure Log Book
**Buyer persona:** Adults 50+ (and their caregivers) managing hypertension, heart disease, or kidney disease who have been told by their doctor to monitor blood pressure at home. They want something simple, easy to read, and organized — not a fancy journal. Family members also buy these as thoughtful, practical gifts for aging parents.
**Core promise:** The simplest, most readable at-home blood pressure log available — large print, dated entries, morning/evening readings, and a pulse column in a clean format seniors can actually use without squinting.
**Format:** 6x9 paperback, 104 pages
**Paperback price:** $6.99
**Kindle price:** $2.99
**Etsy crossover:** NO -- this is a strictly functional log; printable market for this is weak
