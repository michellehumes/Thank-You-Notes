# Cover Design Brief

**KDP trim size:** 6x9
**Page count:** 110
**Spine width formula:** (110 x 0.0025) + 0.06 = 0.335 inches

**Style:** Clean, modern, calm -- conveys medical credibility without being cold. Soft and approachable for someone in chronic pain who wants to feel supported, not pathologized.
**Color palette:**
  - #E8F4F0 Soft Sage (background / front cover wash)
  - #3B7A6B Deep Teal (title text, spine)
  - #C8D8E4 Dusty Blue (accent bars, section dividers)
  - #F7F3EF Warm Off-White (subtitle text block, back cover background)

**Typography:**
  - Title font: Montserrat Bold
  - Subtitle font: Inter Regular

**Cover imagery:** A top-down flat-lay photo of a spiral notebook lying open on a clean white desk, with a fine-tip black pen resting diagonally across the pages. Beside it: a small succulent plant, and a single prescription bottle (cap on, label facing away). Natural side lighting. No people. Calm, organized, hopeful. Suitable search terms for Canva stock: "notebook desk flat lay", "journal pen plant desk", "wellness planner overhead". Alternatively, generate in ChatGPT Images with prompt: "Minimalist flat lay, open journal on white desk, black pen, small green succulent, soft natural light, top-down view, no text, clean editorial style."

**Required text:**
  - Title: Symptom & Pain Tracking Journal
  - Subtitle: A Daily Log for Chronic Illness, Pain Management & Doctor Appointments
  - Author: Shelzy Perkins

**Back cover copy (3-4 sentences):**
Living with chronic illness means your body changes every day -- and your doctor only sees you for 15 minutes. This journal gives you a structured daily log to track pain scores, symptoms, sleep, medications, food triggers, and mood in one place. Arrive at every appointment with real data, ask better questions, and finally show your provider the full picture of how you're living. 110 pages | 6x9 paperback | Shelzy Perkins

**Canva steps:** Search '6x9 book cover' in Canva, select blank template (6 x 9 in). Set front cover background to #E8F4F0. Place stock image in upper 55% of front cover. Add a horizontal color bar in #3B7A6B at 56% from top (1.5 in tall). Set title text in Montserrat Bold 36pt #F7F3EF centered on the color bar. Below bar: subtitle in Inter Regular 14pt #3B7A6B. Author name at bottom in Inter Regular 12pt #3B7A6B. Spine: #3B7A6B background, title rotated 90° in Montserrat Bold 11pt #F7F3EF. Back cover: #F7F3EF background, back cover copy in Inter Regular 10pt #3B7A6B, centered, top third.
