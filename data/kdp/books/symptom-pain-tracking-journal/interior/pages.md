## Page 2: How to Use This Book

```
How to Use This Journal

This journal gives you one two-page spread for each tracking day.
Fill in your symptoms, pain scores, sleep, meals, medications, and
mood each day -- even on "good" days, because patterns hide in the
quiet. Bring this book to every doctor, specialist, or telehealth
appointment so your provider can see exactly what your body has been
doing between visits. Use the Quick Reference Card on the next page
to keep your pain scale consistent across all entries.
```

## Page 3: Quick Reference Card

```
PAIN SCALE REFERENCE

0 -- No pain. Fully functional.
1 -- Minimal. Barely noticeable, no interference.
2 -- Mild. Noticeable but can be ignored during activity.
3 -- Mild-moderate. Distracting; manageable with effort.
4 -- Moderate. Interrupts concentration; hard to ignore.
5 -- Moderate. Cannot ignore; limits some activities.
6 -- Moderate-severe. Affects all daily tasks.
7 -- Severe. Dominates attention; limits most activities.
8 -- Intense. Limits all activity; hard to function.
9 -- Excruciating. Barely able to function at all.
10 -- Worst possible. Unable to function; emergency level.
```
