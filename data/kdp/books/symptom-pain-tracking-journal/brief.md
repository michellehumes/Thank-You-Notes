# Book Brief: Symptom & Pain Tracking Journal (chronic illness)

**Niche:** Symptom & Pain Tracking Journal (chronic illness)
**Buyer persona:** Adults living with fibromyalgia, lupus, chronic back pain, Crohn's disease, MS, or other long-term conditions who need a structured system to document daily symptoms, pain levels, and triggers before doctor appointments. They are frustrated that generic notebooks don't have the right fields, and they want to show their provider a clear pattern over time. Purchased for self-use or gifted by a caregiver.
**Core promise:** A medically minded daily log built around the real questions your doctor asks -- so you arrive at every appointment with answers, not guesses.
**Format:** 6x9 paperback, 110 pages
**Paperback price:** $8.99
**Kindle price:** $4.99
**Etsy crossover:** YES -- a printable version of the daily spread sells well on Etsy; buyers want to try before they commit to a bound book.
