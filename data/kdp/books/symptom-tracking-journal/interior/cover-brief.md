# Cover Design Brief: Daily Symptom Tracker

**KDP trim size:** 6x9
**Page count:** 110
**Spine width formula:** (110 x 0.0025) + 0.06 = 0.335 inches

**Style:** Clean, clinical-soft — calm and organized without being cold. Think "I trust this book with my health data."
**Color palette:**
  - `#4A7FA5` — Muted teal-blue (primary, trust/health)
  - `#F5F0E8` — Warm off-white (background, approachable)
  - `#D4E6EF` — Pale sky blue (accent panels)
  - `#2C3E50` — Deep navy (typography contrast)

**Typography:**
  - Title font: Montserrat Bold
  - Subtitle font: Inter Regular

**Cover imagery:** A flat-lay style illustration (not photo-realistic) showing an open journal with neat handwritten-style lines, a small pen, and a minimal heart-rate or pulse line graphic element. Soft, uncluttered. No stock-photo faces. Could also use a clean abstract watercolor wash in teal and white with the title overlaid. Avoid anything that looks "medical" or clinical — this should feel like a self-care tool, not a hospital form.

**Required text:**
  - Title: Daily Symptom Tracker
  - Subtitle: A Daily Log for Chronic Illness, Medical Appointments & Health Patterns
  - Author: Shelzy Perkins

**Canva steps:**
1. Search "6x9 book cover" in Canva, select blank template sized 6x9 inches (front cover only for digital mock; full wrap for KDP upload).
2. Set background to `#F5F0E8`.
3. Add a teal (`#4A7FA5`) banner stripe across the top (~1.5 inches tall).
4. Place title "Daily Symptom Tracker" in Montserrat Bold, `#2C3E50`, centered, large (approx. 48–52pt).
5. Add subtitle in Inter Regular, `#2C3E50`, centered below title (approx. 18pt).
6. Place cover illustration/watercolor element in the center third of the cover.
7. Add "Shelzy Perkins" in Inter Regular, small (14pt), bottom center, `#4A7FA5`.
8. For spine: `#4A7FA5` background, "Daily Symptom Tracker — Shelzy Perkins" in white Montserrat Bold rotated 90°.
9. Back cover: `#D4E6EF` background, brief 3-line description, barcode placeholder bottom right.
10. Export full wrap as PDF Print (with bleeds) for KDP upload.
