# Book Brief: Symptom Tracking Journal

**Niche:** Symptom Tracking Journal
**Buyer persona:** Adults living with chronic illness (fibromyalgia, Lupus, MS, Lyme disease, long COVID, or undiagnosed conditions) who need to bring organized, date-stamped symptom data to doctor appointments. They're often frustrated by being dismissed and want concrete evidence of patterns. They buy repeatedly as they fill books quickly.
**Core promise:** A structured daily log that captures symptoms, triggers, medications, sleep, and pain levels in one place — so patients walk into appointments with data instead of guesses.
**Format:** 6x9 paperback, 110 pages
**Paperback price:** $7.99
**Kindle price:** $3.99
**Etsy crossover:** YES -- printable version sells well to the chronic illness community on Etsy; can be offered as a digital download
