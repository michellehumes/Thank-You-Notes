## Status Legend
- `queued` -- scored and ready to design
- `in-progress` -- prompts generated, art being created
- `uploaded` -- all designs live on platforms
- `reviewing` -- checking sales data after 30 days
- `expand` -- winner niche, build 10+ more designs in this niche
- `retire` -- low/no sales after 6 months, stop adding to this niche
